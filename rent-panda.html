background.

Rent Panda is an online rental platform that stretches across Ontario. They are progressively becoming the go to place for renters and rentees alike. Recently, the start-up decided to re-develop their website from scratch, improving greatly upon its functionality. With new features like tenant and landlord verification, in app communication, secure rent payment gateway and a whole lot more, rent panda is defining what it means to rent in Ontario.

tasks completed.

  1. Developed and presented a creative direction that would set the grounds for every deliverable moving forward.
  2. Created a suite of assets to be dripped into various networks such as social media, paid online ads and print materials.
  3. Performed an audit of the current Figma boards for the platform and noted all aspects that needed improving..
  4. Re-designed all new landing and informational pages using Adobe XD.
  5. Improved upon the current dashboard, consolidating all new icons, buttons and states.
  6. Communication with developers, ensuring a seamless handoff of all designs.
